# Data Strategy

## Common Engineering Practice for Data Strategy

It is essential for a product to understand the data it will consume, create, edit, and delete. Platform+  is the data hub for all Platform+ data. As part of being that hub, Platform+  needs to get the data others need, get the data that is created by Platform+ products, and prepare it for others to use.

As teams go through the Kanban process, a data strategy is part of the agreements.

### Discovery & Framing Exit Criteria

- The product team should be determining high-level data they need and how the product will judge the success of their product.

### Pre-Launch Exit Criteria

- The product team needs to identify where they are getting data (usually Platform+ ) and what data they are creating, updating, and deleting for both transactional and analytical data.
- Data retention should be defined for the data.
- This plan needs to be reviewed with the Platform+  team. The earlier the product team reviews the data needs with Platform+  and gets it into the backlog (if necessary), the better Platform+  can meet the team's deadlines.

### Launch and Beyond

- The product team should keep the conversation up with the Platform+  team as data changes, either data that is needed or data that is created, updated, or deleted by the product.
:::info
The product team should also document data that it creates and decided not to retain in case it could be considered for use in the future.
:::
