## Protech Analytics & AI Anchor Responsiblities

* **COP Participation**: Attend Anchor COP meetings as a priority.
* **Manifesto Contribution**: Contribute to Analytics and AI manifestos.
* **Team Leadership**: Provide stability and technical leadership for assigned team.
* **Matched Pair Collaboration**: Work as a Matched Pair with the Product PM.
* **GTM Support**: Provide technical leadership for Go-To-Market initiatives.
* **Operating Model Alignment**: Align with Product Group Architect (PGA) on processes and practices as defined by the Operating Model.
* **Tech Council Preparation**: Prepare for Tech Council meetings and presentations.
* **Product Team Newsie pages** updated with **current Architecture**
* **Exit Agreement Management**: Prepare and manage Tech Council exit agreements.
* **SLO Onboarding**: Onboard to Service Level Objectives (SLOs) for Quality of Service (QoS) based on technical solutions.
* **OIC Control Oversight**: Ensure adherence to Operational Integrity Control (OIC) processes for the team, in collaboration with the PM.
* **Best Practice Establishment**: Help establish best practices for technical solutions within the Product Group.
* **People mover and Roster** tutorial for new technical team members.
