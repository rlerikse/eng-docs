# Team Ceremonies: Fostering Collaboration and Efficiency
This section details the Product Manager's role in facilitating key team ceremonies to ensure effective collaboration, efficient execution, and continuous improvement.

## Daily Stand-ups: Focus and Alignment
Daily stand-ups, often facilitated by the Product Manager, are concise and focused on ensuring team alignment and proactive problem-solving. The primary goals are to confirm we're working on the highest priority items and identify any roadblocks or areas where team members need assistance. Open communication and knowledge sharing are encouraged. The focus is on collaboration and proactive problem-solving, avoiding micromanagement.

## Iteration Planning: Defining Success
The Product Manager leads iteration planning sessions, guiding the team in defining achievable goals aligned with the product strategy. This involves prioritizing tasks based on value, dependencies, and available resources, creating a realistic and actionable plan for the upcoming iteration.

## Sprint Reviews (or Demos): Showcasing Value
[Working software is the primary measure of progress](https://agilemanifesto.org/principles.html), demonstrated in sprint reviews (demos). The Product Manager collaborates with the development team to create compelling presentations showcasing completed work and user value. Effective demos are concise, focused on completed user stories, encourage active participation and feedback, use real-world examples, and highlight key features and benefits. Visual aids illustrate impact, and feedback informs future sprints, culminating in a celebration of team achievements.

## Team Health Checks
The Product Manager facilitates quarterly Team Health Checks using [Vibez](https://vibez.company.com/) to assess team morale, identify obstacles to productivity and collaboration, and proactively address emerging concerns. Unlike iteration-specific retrospectives, these check-ins monitor overall team well-being. Vibez provides built-in and customizable prompts, with a single facilitator guiding the discussion and action planning. The focus is on identifying issues; resolution happens after the check-in. Progress is tracked through quarterly comparisons of results. See the [VIBEZ FAQ](https://vibez.company.com/frequently-asked-questions) for more details.
