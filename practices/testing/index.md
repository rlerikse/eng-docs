## Benefits of working with Platform Validation team
* Through exploratory testing and working closely with product teams, Platform Validation have been supporting multiple product launches with minimal critical defects
* Platform Validation support testing on a variety of devices and browsers for frontend products 
* Product teams can receive quick feedback on implemented features as well as UI and UX
* Frontend product teams benefit most from Platform Validation with various devices and browsers support; Backend and API product teams will benefit as well from our exploratory testing practices
