## About
Platform Validation is a product team within the Platform Services Product Group, Platform Enablement Product Line. The 
team provides testing support to individual product teams within Platform+ Tech as well as the Platform+ Tech platform. 

Exploratory Testing is a testing practice within the Platform Validation team. It is a form of manual testing and is the 
initial step to engage with other product teams to provide testing support and feedback and to uncover potential 
unforeseen defects. The team complements other product teams to deliver products with efficiency and high quality. 

Platform Validation team members are embedded into the product teams that they support. 

## Benefits
* Through exploratory testing and working closely with product teams, Platform Validation have been supporting multiple 
product launches with minimal critical defects
* Platform Validation support testing on a variety of devices and browsers for frontend products 
* Product teams can receive quick feedback on implemented features as well as UI and UX 
* Frontend product teams benefit most from Platform Validation with various devices and browsers support; Backend and API 
product teams will benefit as well from our exploratory testing practices 
* For more details about the testing process, how Platform Validation can help, the type of support provided and how to 
approach the team, please reference the [Platform Validation Testing Process document](https://sharepoint.company.com/:w:/r/sites/EnterpriseTech/_layouts/15/doc2.aspx?sourcedoc=%7B00386a14-5c72-432e-b88a-beaf877b6fb4%7D&action=edit&cid=18b15835-f69d-4a75-8c6b-53312678500c)

## Testing Focus
Platform Validation currently is focused on product team testing support. In the long run, Platform Validation aims to 
pivot to support the overall Platform+ platform, aside from individual product team support. However, we understand there 
are many types/forms of testing and below are the testing focus of the team: 

### Feature Verification 
* Platform Validation ensures delivered stories are implemented as per the product requirement and user acceptance criteria
* Exploratory testing is performed around the implemented features to determine if any unforeseen flaws might have been overlooked during feature development

### Defect Verification 
* Platform Validation ensures delivered defect correctly addresses the original defect and the defect is no longer reproducible 
* Exploratory testing is performed around the implemented defect fix to determine if any other defects could potentially be introduced

### Accessibility Testing
* We have accessibility testing support available for web and mobile products 
* Accessibility test cases are created for each product and validated against the WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines 
* Accessibility testing utilizes tools such as Narrator and NVDA to ensure our product adheres to the standards

### Analytics Testing (high level)
* High level analytics support is provided, i.e., Platform Validation tests to ensure certain events will be fired upon performing a certain user action 
* Platform Validation does not verify all analytics event though as the detailed analytics testing is done by a separate analytics testing team 

### Performance Testing (not in scope for Platform Validation) 
* Platform Validation does not provide performance testing support, this is something that can be performed from the development team through the integration of performance testing tools with the product codebase 
* Platform Validation team can only provide general performance feedback based on team member’s experience of product performance from testing of various delivered builds
