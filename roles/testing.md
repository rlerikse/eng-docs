## Test Engineer
* Verify that user stories meet the defined acceptance criteria
* Perform exploratory testing with the use of Charters, to unearth bugs and potential problems with the product before they reach production
* Assist in developing and execution of test plans for a product’s release
* Automate smoke/release candidate test cases to quickly determine the stability of the product and provide a faster feedback loop

## Test Engineer Anchor
* Define the scope of exploratory and automated testing for a product, and establish a testing cadence
* Keep the team focused on ensuring that delivered user stories meet the defined acceptance criteria
* Leads the team in exploratory testing with the use of Charters
* Spread product knowledge by pair testing with other test engineers
* Communicates with product managers and other stakeholders on product updates, defects and potential risks
